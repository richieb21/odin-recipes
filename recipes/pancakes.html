Pancake Recipe

Pancakes are a delicious breakfast item that resemebles a flatter cake. It is often served with maple syrup and butter to hit that sweet craving of yours!

INGREDIENTS:

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup miniature chocolate chips
  • Cooking spray

    • DIRECTIONS:

    1. Combine milk and vinegar in a bowl
    2. Combine dry ingredients in another bowl (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt)
    3. Whisk egg, butter and vanilla extact into the milk and vinegar
    4. Pour the flour mixture into the wet ingredients and whisk until combined
    5. Fold in chocolate chips
    6. Heat a large skillet, add cooking spray and add about 1/4 cup batter for each pancake
    7. Cook until bubbles appear on surface and flip with spatula
    8. Serve with butter and maple syrup and enjoy
